Know Your Legal Rights

Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act, 2013

Comprehensive law protecting women from sexual harassment at workplace.

  • Every workplace must have an Internal Committee (IC)
  • Complaints can be filed within 3 months
  • False complaints can have consequences
  • Employers must ensure safety

Equal Remuneration Act, 1976

Ensures equal pay for equal work regardless of gender.

  • Equal pay required
  • Prohibition of discrimination in recruitment
  • Right to file complaints
  • Penalties for violations
